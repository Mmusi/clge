About Us

CLGE works with individuals and businesses using transformative leadership development and social change mechanisms to unravel foundational barriers that impede personal & collective functioning including socio-cultural, gender conceptions, economic and entrepreneurial technical capacity.

As a Consortium, our innovative approach taps into the strength of international strategic alliances that we forge to handle entrepreneurial and social challenges. GLGE vests special interest in the discourse of African women's, men’s, girls’ and boys’ empowerment using gender harmonious initiatives to bridge the gender parity gap for equitable prosperity in business and social development at all levels.

Entrepreneurial Advisory
