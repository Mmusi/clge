About Us

CLGE works with individuals and businesses using transformative leadership development and social change mechanisms to unravel and address foundational barriers (such as socio-cultural, gender conceptions, lack of economic and technical entrepreneurial capacity) constraining personal & collective performance. It helps companies develop brands, accelerate and expand, and market them in Africa and beyond.

As a Consortium, our innovative approach relies on forging international strategic alliances to offer the best solutions. GLGE vests special interest in the discourse of African women's, men’s, girls’ and boys’ empowerment using gender harmonious initiatives to bridge the gender parity gap for equitable prosperity in business and social development at all levels.

Our Services

Social Development Advocacy Research & Dialogue

We host dialogue platforms to engage various parties in conversations collecting real-time data to investigate and find evidence-based solutions to topical social, business and development issues. We leverage the project partnership approach to get the best results for our clients and the greatest impact from diverse inputs! We use:


• Personal & Group Engagements

• Organizational Seminars, Workshops & Discussion Panels

• International, Regional & National Conferences, Symposia & Colloquia

Leadership & Development Management Advisory

•Personal development coaching, counselling, study guidance, job shadowing, exchange experiences, talent search, motivation, assessment/reviews, change management

• Research, capacity building, assessments / reviews, curriculum development, policy analysis & advisory (concept notes & policy briefs) etc.







Entrepreneurial Advisory

Our entrepreneurial advisory services seek to explore, open and extend business development and market access opportunities, and connect local industry with regional and international counterparts facilitating participation in available in trade arrangements e.g. the African Continental Free Trade Area (AfCFTA), African Growth and Opportunity Act (AGOA) etc.


• Spotlighting Inter-regional / International Trade Arrangements

• Availing Regional / International Networking Chances to Local SMEs

• Business Incubation for Informal/ Marginalized Sectors
